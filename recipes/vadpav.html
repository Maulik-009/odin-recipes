Vadapav

Description

This is a spicy dish with Bread(Pav) with a Potato patty and usually served with a Green chilli

Ingredients

  • Pav
  • Batata Vada
  • Green Chutney
  • Green Chilli
  • Garlic Chutney

Steps

  1. Grab some pavs from the market
  2. Boil some potatos
  3. Make the batter
  4. Dip the potatos in the batter and make Batata Vada
  5. Make the Green Chutney
  6. Make the Garlic Chutney
  7. Assemble the Vada Pav
