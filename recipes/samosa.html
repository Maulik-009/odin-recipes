Samosa

Description

A Poatato stuffed deep fried snack usually eaten with chutney

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Boiled Potato
  • Cumin Seeds
  • Chutney

Steps

  1. Mix up the flour
  2. Boil some potatos
  3. Make the potatos into masala (Make it spicier)
  4. Add the Cumin seeds
  5. Make the Chutney
  6. Fry the Samosas
